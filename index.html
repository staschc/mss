Meaningful Spatial Statistics

This portal allows you to annotate datasets for spatial statistical analysis in order to provide additional information whether the dataset is a point pattern, geostatistical data, lattice data, or trajectory data. Depending on the type of data, statistical procedures such as spatial interpolation are meaningful or not. To find out more about the different data types and meaninfgul statistical analysis, click below.
